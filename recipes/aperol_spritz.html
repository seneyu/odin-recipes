Frozen Aperol Spritz

Description

If a spritz is one of your go-to cocktails then you will love how similar this frozen version tastes. This frozen cocktail is ready in a total of 10 mintues, making it a great last minute drink idea or an easy idea to keep in mind on a hot day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mango frozen
  • 6 ounces Aperol
  • 2 ounces prosecco
  • 2 ounces club soda
  • juice of half an orange
  • 2 cups ice
  • orange or clemetine slices for garnish

Steps

  1. Add mango and Aperol to a blender and blend until mango is just pureed.
  2. Add prosecco, club soda, and orange juice to the blender, blending until just incorporated.
  3. Add ice and blend until just crushed.
  4. Divide between two cocktail glasses and top with orange or clementine slices if desired. Drink immediately and enjoy!
